Hello, wolfs!

Welcome to the FluffyLand! Our website about wolves... our favorite fluffy howling animals! :3

The wolves are carnivorous animals that eat mostly deer, elk and rabbit. They are shy about humans and wolf attacks are actually pretty rare.

These fluffy guys have a thick coat of fur to protect them from the cold winter and harsh environment!

We love anthro (or furry) wolves! This website itself is made by furries :)
